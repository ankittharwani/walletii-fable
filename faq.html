FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything about getting started, sending money, fees, security, and rewards — searchable in seconds.

🔎

How can I sign up and start using walletii?Getting started+
Download the walletii app, and it will guide you through the sign-up process. We have to verify your identity before you can send or receive money — you'll be asked to take a selfie and scan your ID, so have it at hand.
Am I eligible to open a walletii account?Getting started+
To open a walletii account, you must be over 18, hold a valid Omani ID (identity card or resident card), and have an Omani phone number. You can also hold multiple wallets with different providers at the same time.
How does my walletii account become activated?Getting started+
Once you've finished the quick verification process, your account usually opens within a couple of minutes. Occasionally we may need to do additional identity checks, which take 1–3 business days.
Will I be charged for opening walletii?Getting started+
No — opening a walletii wallet is free, and you won't be charged for keeping a zero balance or being inactive.
Why can't I find walletii on the App Store or Google Play?Getting started+
Make sure you're searching the App Store or Google Play of a country where walletii is available. You can always switch your store location.
Is the walletii app available for HarmonyOS?Getting started+
The walletii app is only available for iOS and Android.
What is an alias?My profile & account+
An alias is a unique identifier for your wallet that you can share to receive money from any other wallet or bank. All walletii aliases end in @WLTI. You're given a random alias at sign-up, but we recommend changing the part before the @ to something memorable, in settings.
What is a 'default wallet', and how do I set it up?My profile & account+
Your default wallet receives any money sent to your phone number. To make walletii your default, go to your profile and enable the 'Set as default wallet' switch. To make another wallet the default, set it from that wallet.
I have forgotten my passcode. What can I do?My profile & account+
Use the forgotten-passcode feature: we'll take a selfie to verify your identity, then you can create a new passcode.
What if I lose my phone?My profile & account+
Download walletii on your new phone and log in with your existing number. We'll verify your phone number and passcode, which automatically signs out the account on the lost phone.
What if I want to change my phone number?My profile & account+
For security reasons, the phone number linked to your account can't be changed. You'll need to close your existing account and open a new one with your new number.
My ID has expired — what should I do?My profile & account+
Log in to the app and you'll be prompted to update your personal details, where you can upload a new ID. You're good to go once it's verified.
How do I close my walletii account?My profile & account+
Go to profile settings → Account details → Close account. Make sure your balance is 0 OMR first — otherwise we can't close it. Closures are processed within two weeks.
How can I add money to my wallet?Adding money+
Three ways: use your bank card via 'Add money' in the app; send money from a local bank account to your walletii alias (found in Profile); or send from any other local wallet.
Will I be charged for adding money?Adding money+
No — adding money is always free.
Can I use a non-Omani card to add money?Adding money+
No, only Omani debit or prepaid cards are accepted.
Is there a limit to how much money I can add?Adding money+
You can add up to 500 OMR in a single top-up, with a daily limit and maximum balance of 1,000 OMR.
Can I send money abroad using walletii?International payments+
Yes — to over 200 countries. Enter the amount, pick a delivery method, fill in the recipient's details, confirm, and the money is on its way. We'll keep you updated until it arrives.
What delivery options are available?International payments+
You can send to bank accounts, cash pick-up points, wallets abroad, or any Visa or Mastercard payment card. Available options vary by country and are shown when you choose the destination.
How long do international transactions take?International payments+
Cash pick-ups are ready to collect in 10 minutes or less. Bank transfers usually arrive the next day, depending on the local bank. We'll keep you updated throughout.
Can I send money to India using UPI?International payments+
Yes — walletii supports sending to India via UPI, making transfers home faster and easier.
Why do I see more options to send money to one country?International payments+
Walletii lets you select both the delivery method and the provider, so you can choose based on your preferred rate, fee, speed, or delivery method.
Is there a limit on international transfers?International payments+
Yes — 1,000 OMR per single transaction and per day, and 15,000 OMR per month across all outgoing transactions.
Can I transfer money between walletii and bank accounts?Local payments+
Yes. To receive from a bank, share your walletii alias (in Profile) and use it in your banking app. To send to a bank, add a new local recipient in 'Send money' and enter your bank account's alias.
Can I send money to other mobile wallets?Local payments+
Yes — to any local wallet, instantly. You only need the recipient's phone number or alias.
How fast are the transfers?Local payments+
Local payments are instant — the recipient gets the money immediately.
Is there a limit to how much money I can send locally?Local payments+
Yes — up to 500 OMR per day.
What is 'request money', and how can I use it?Local payments+
Tap 'Request money', enter the amount, and share it with a saved contact or as a QR code through other apps. The QR can be scanned by walletii 'Scan and pay' or other local wallets.
What should I do if I get a payment request?Local payments+
You'll get a notification and an in-app banner. Open it to confirm or decline — you have 2 minutes before the request expires. Always check you recognise the person and the amount.
How can I pay at stores using walletii?Local payments+
In most stores that accept cards, ask for a QR code at checkout and use 'Scan and pay'. Some merchants may share their phone number for payment instead.
How can I withdraw money?Local payments+
Simply send the money to another wallet or to a bank account.
Are there any transaction fees?Rates & charges+
Sending within Oman is free. International transfers may carry a fee depending on the destination, provider, and delivery option, plus VAT where applicable — always shown clearly in the app before you confirm.
Will I be charged for receiving money?Rates & charges+
No — receiving money into walletii is free.
Do I need to maintain a minimum balance?Rates & charges+
No minimum balance is required. You can keep it at 0 — we won't charge you for inactivity.
Is there a maximum balance for my account?Rates & charges+
Yes — your wallet balance cannot exceed 1,000 OMR.
Is walletii safe?Security & fraud+
Yes. We use the latest technology to keep your money secure and comply with all relevant Omani and international security standards. We also choose partners like MoneyGram for their proven approach to financial and data security.
How does walletii protect my account?Security & fraud+
Your account is secured with a passcode or biometrics. We constantly monitor for suspicious activity and act immediately when needed. Official updates only ever come through walletii channels.
What can I do to keep my account safe?Security & fraud+
Choose a strong, unique passcode and change it regularly. Don't reuse your phone-unlock code, never share or write down your passcode, lock your device, enable biometrics, and review your transactions often. Suspect fraud? Call +968-80008880 or email support.om@walletii.com.
How can I identify and avoid fraud?Security & fraud+
Watch for unexpected prize or job messages, or ads claiming you've won money from walletii or its partners. Be cautious of anyone asking to use your wallet for transfers. If something feels off, verify in the app or contact us directly.
What should I do if I suspect fraud?Security & fraud+
In the app, go to Help & Support → Contact Us → Message Us, or call +968-80008880. Never reply to unknown messages or share your passcode.
What bills and services can I pay with walletii?Bills & recharges+
Airtime for Ooredoo, Omantel, Vodafone, FRiENDi, Renna and RedBull Mobile; postpaid mobile, internet and landline bills; water and electricity (including prepaid); education bills at selected schools and universities; government bills including ROP traffic tickets; financial services; media services; and charity donations.
Is there a limit to how much I can pay or recharge?Bills & recharges+
Up to 500 OMR per day. Minimums and maximums vary by biller and service and are shown in the app.
How can I check the status of my bill payment?Bills & recharges+
Go to 'History', tap the arrow at the bottom, then tap the transaction. Note: a payment can show as successful in walletii while the biller takes a few hours to reflect the updated balance.
My recharge or bill payment hasn't reached the biller yet — what's going on?Bills & recharges+
Prepaid payments are usually instant; postpaid bills can take a few hours. If it's been more than 24 hours, contact us via 'Help & support' in the app and we'll look into it right away.
Where can I find the recharge PIN for prepaid electricity?Bills & recharges+
The PIN is sent by SMS to the phone number on your walletii profile. If it doesn't arrive, contact us through 'Help and support' in the app.
Can I pay a bill for a family member?Bills & recharges+
Yes — you can pay bills for others if you know their mobile phone number.
How can I invite a friend?Rewards & referrals+
Go to 'Invite friends' for your unique referral code and share it. When your friend creates their account, they enter it under 'Invite friends' → 'I have a referral code'.
I didn't get a referral reward.Rewards & referrals+
You receive the reward once your friend makes their first payment. If they have and your reward hasn't arrived within 10 minutes, contact us via 'Help and support' in the app.
Can I use walletii outside of Oman?Getting started+
Yes, you can use the app abroad just as you would in Oman. However, new users cannot download and sign up while outside the country.
My verification is pending — what does it mean?My profile & account+
We need a little more time to verify your identity. You'll be notified as soon as the process completes — thanks for your patience.
My documents have been rejected. What now?My profile & account+
Some of your personal data may have been assessed as incorrect. Try the verification process again, or contact us through 'Help and support' in the app.
I'm having issues scanning my ID — what do I do?My profile & account+
Clean your ID and try different lighting. If the problem persists, contact us through 'Help and support' in the app.
