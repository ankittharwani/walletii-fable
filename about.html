About us

Our story

More than a place to keep your money — a gateway to financial empowerment and inclusion, for individuals and merchants alike.

The beginning of a promise

It started with an ambitious mission

In 2022, we set ourselves a goal: to transform the way people and businesses manage their finances across the Middle East and North Africa. Walletii is the result.

And it's for everyone — not just Ooredoo customers. Enjoy the peace of mind that comes from safe, reliable money transfers, with full visibility of your money every step of the way.

Our mission

Make your financial journey smooth and uneventful

We financially empower individuals and businesses with intuitive tools and a supportive community — all backed by the trusted Ooredoo group, and built in partnership with leading financial and technology experts.
